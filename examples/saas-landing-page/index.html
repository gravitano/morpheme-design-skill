Features

Everything your team needs to move fast

Flowi brings your tasks, automations, and integrations together — no duct tape required.

Smart Automations

Build no-code automation flows in minutes. Trigger actions across 200+ tools when conditions are met — no engineering help needed.

Unified Task Board

Kanban, list, calendar, and timeline — switch views without losing context. One source of truth for every project, every team.

Real-time Analytics

Track velocity, cycle time, and team throughput in real time. Spot bottlenecks before they become blockers with built-in reports.

Integrated Docs & Chat

Write specs, share updates, and discuss tasks in context — not buried in Slack threads. Every comment is attached to the right work item.

Enterprise Security

SSO, SAML, role-based permissions, audit logs, and SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure. Your data never leaves your control.

200+ Integrations

GitHub, Slack, Jira, Figma, Linear, Google Drive, Notion — connect your stack in one click and keep everything in sync, always.
